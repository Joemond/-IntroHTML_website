How to sleep at night©

This is not the only method! There are other ways to do this successfully.

Materials needed: Bed(pillow, mattress, blanket are all essential into making a bed)

List

  • First, be in your house
  • Second, get in your bed. If you do not have a bed, the floor will suffice
  • Third, get your pillow. The pillow is essential when needing to sleep. Rest your head on it.
  • Fourth, get your blanket. The pillow is essential when needing to sleep. Put it over your body, but NOT the toes.
  • Lastly, close the lights. Then rest

Nighty night!
